About StressMap

StressMap is a stress prediction platform created by Poorvi Taranagar and Dewansh, built to help students and young professionals understand and manage exam and work-related stress.

This is a self-reflection tool, not a medical diagnosis. If things ever feel heavy, please talk to a trusted adult, teacher, or counsellor.
